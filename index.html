Welcome to Vikens Kennel!

My kennel conducts a small and dedicated breeding of Rhodesian Ridgeback.
My ambition is to breed healthy & nice dogs,
who, above all, should function as family members in their new homes.
Are you thinking of getting a Rhodesian Ridgeback,
then you are most welcome to contact me
with your questions or for general "dog talk".
Of course I am a member of: The Swedish Kennel Club!
